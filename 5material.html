Cooking New Material


Material developer: Youyang Song


„Cooking New Material“ is based on a self-developed process that allows organic waste to be processed into flexible, leather-like materials. Banana and orange peel or soy milk pomace are combined with a natural binder as a matrix. the resulting composite material can be easily reused after „re-cooking“. It is 100% biodegradable with „zero waste“.
