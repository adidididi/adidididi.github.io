Neos -
Tangible Apps Kit


Material: NFC, Arduino, Automator
Supervised by Prof. Carola Zwick and Prof. Dr. Jörg Petruschat.


Bored and tired of typing and wiping on screens? What is the difference between access to payment and access to the camera? Neos brings orientation back into everyday life. Neos consists of five different components for contacts, calls, photos, music and payment. They create logical links to digital technologies in an intuitive, physically comprehensible way. Various areas of the digital world are open to us. Our interactions with it will be easier, more transparent and more playful - without any smartphones at all.
