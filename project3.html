Qiro


Teamwork: Simon von Schmude, Ningyuan Xu
Material: Transducer, Amplifier, Poti, Bluetooth, NFC

Qiro is a puppet shaped speaker could be turned on and set to a volume by just opening its „mouth“. This metaphor can then be used for other interactive gestures, like the mute function.


A speaker comes to life by gestures,
allowing more sensible and plausible interactions.
