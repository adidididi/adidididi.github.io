The multichain wallet

LongHash Hackathon 1st Prize Winner
Credit to: Xinyue Yang

Wookong Wallet is a powerful and secure cross-chain wallet, it supports decentralized cross-chain trading, and creates a ecosystem.

A Private key is the one and only ‘Ticket’ to your crypto asset. The hardware wallet is a secure device to store your private key which makes it never touch the potentially vulnerable environment. It means your private key need not be transferred out of the device in plain- text to any other insecure device and is immune to computer viruses that may steal your crypto assets.
