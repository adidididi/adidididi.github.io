21C. Stationary
21C. Stationary is a tangible application tool kit, it contains five main components: Contacts, Call, Camera, Play and Pay. By physically controlling these components, logical shortcuts to the digital world will be created. Users can make calls, take photos, play music, make payment……，without smart phones. Tired of tapping and digging on your phone? Annoyed that voice assistant couldn’t understand you? 21C. Stationary connect the digital and analog world by using gestures to support users intuition, without techie knowledge, without thinking, without time wasting. It brings the tangible interaction back to our daily life, helps the user to interact with the technology more intuitively.
