Mirror


Material: Arduino, Ultrasonic Sensor, Motor
Supervised by Prof. Mika Satomi


This is a smart mirror which continuously adjusts its zoom ratio, by sensing how far you are from it. One step forward to zoom in and focus on putting on your makeup. One step backwards to see your full body for getting dressed. There you have both a makeup mirror and a full legnth mirror in a limited space.
