Bose Advanced XD Team


I worked at Bose Experience Design Team as an interaction designer. The experience team’s functions therefore go beyond standard product development. It is focusing on the top for customer experience, interface design, wearable device concept, and mixed reality concept, for the general listening experience of the future. During my time there, I focused on designing the user experience for open ear devices, for example the Bose soundwear family products. and the user experience for noise-cancelling devices, including bose sleepbuds series.

The content here does not involve any specific research result or product solutions, most of the content only refers to general areas and my personal views.
