Sound Jacket


Teamwork: Ylenia Gortana , Ningyuan Xu
Material: Conductive Wool, e-Textile, Arduino, Pure-Data


Both elbows of the jacket are filled with conductive wool. As the conductive wool were compressed, so changed its resistant. That’s how the body motion alters sound output.
